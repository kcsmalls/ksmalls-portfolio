About me

I am a technical writer with experience creating clear, concise content for both hardware and software applications. I produce and manage documentation projects across Agile and Waterfall environments, ensuring high-quality deliverables that meet user needs and project goals.

My background includes developing end-user documentation from requirements, specifications, and subject matter expert interviews, as well as applying a Docs-as-Code approach to streamline content creation and management. Skilled at multi-tasking, I have successfully overseen multiple documentation projects simultaneously while maintaining accuracy, consistency, and usability.
